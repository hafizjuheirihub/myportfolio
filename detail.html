OWNER FOUNDER

Navwinderan A/L Mahenderan, the visionary founder of Pawtarian, has been at the forefront of the organization's mission to advocate for the rights of street dogs in Malaysia. His passion for animal welfare and his unwavering dedication have been instrumental in driving the organization forward. With a deep-seated belief in the values of respect, responsibility, and compassion towards all living beings, Navwinderan has worked tirelessly to create a platform that inspires individuals to become agents of change, love, and compassion within their communities.


Pawtarian formally known as Persatuan Pembelaan Hak Anjing Jalan yang Tersisih Selangor (PAWTARIAN), is a non-governmental organization (NGO) which based in Klang, Selangor. Established in 2018 by the owner founder, Pawtarian has been dedicated to transforming the lives of street dogs and the communities they inhabit since 2013. Other than street dogs, Pawtarian also take in street cats to take good care of their welfare before opening up to adoption to find a new family. Despite facing numerous challenges, the organization has remained steadfast in its commitment to animal welfare, focusing not only on rescues and adoptions but also on sterilization, vaccinations, and educational initiatives.




OUR WORKS

ADOPTION DRIVE WITH TEAMS
Pawtarian Sunday Adoption Drive was born in Bangsar. We now run weekly adoption drive on Sundays at IOI MALL, Puchong and other locations n Klang Valley. Through our Adoption Drive, we have successfully rehomed 400+ cats and dogs. The adoption drive draws pet lovers from both near and far, matching homeless puppies and adult dogs with suitable, loving families. This event can reduce the population of street pets.
RESCUE AND TREATMENT
Pawtarian is dedicated to helping animals in need of medical care. We rescue and provide attention to these animals, ensuring they receive the necessary treatment from veterinarians. Through our social media platform, we raise funds for their medical bills with the help of generous donors. Once these animals are healthy and ready, they are placed for adoption, giving them a second chance at a loving home.
TRAP NEUTER AND RELEASE
Pawtarian is dedicated to responsible TNR (Trap-Neuter and Return) efforts. We collaborate closely with local caregivers, trapping stray dogs for sterilization. After their visit to the vet, they are cared for and observed for 2-3 days before being returned to their familiar environment. This approach aims to reduce the stray population and ensures these dogs continue to receive care from dedicated feeders.

Let's adopt with us

Saving one animal will not change the world, but surely for that one animal, the world will change forever. By choosing to adopt, you are not just providing a home; you are giving that animal a second chance at life, love, and happiness. Every adoption is a victory against the suffering and neglect that so many animals face. While it may seem like a small act in the grand scheme of things, for that one animal, it means everything. You are their hero, their savior, and their new beginning. The ripple effect of this one compassionate decision can inspire others to do the same, gradually making a significant difference in the world of animal welfare.
